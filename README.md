## 🪷 About Me

I'm a Computer Science and Engineering student at **Daffodil International University**, currently building my foundation in programming, web development, and problem solving. I got into coding through **CS Academy**, and I've been steadily expanding from language basics into building small, complete projects — games, interactive tools, and web-based experiments — rather than only studying theory.

What drives me is less about writing code for its own sake, and more about **making something a person can actually open and use** — whether that's a chatbot, a browser game, or a hardware project like a line-following car. I care about finishing things, keeping them simple, and understanding *why* something works instead of just copying a pattern.

I'm still early in my engineering journey, and I'm approaching it with curiosity rather than overconfidence — learning full-stack fundamentals, exploring how AI tools fit into a developer's workflow, and figuring out what kind of engineer I want to become.

**Open To:**
- 🎓 Software engineering internships
- 🤝 Open-source collaboration and small dev teams
- 🧪 Beginner-friendly AI/ML learning projects
- 💻 Freelance web/front-end work
- 🌍 Remote and campus-based opportunities
- 🏆 Student leadership and campus ambassador programs
